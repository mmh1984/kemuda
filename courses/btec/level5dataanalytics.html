Pearson BTEC HND Level 5 in Computing (Major in Data Analytics)

The Pearson BTEC Level 5 HND Diploma in Computing and Systems Development (QCF) provides opportunities for learners to apply their knowledge and practical skills in the workplace. Full-time learners have the opportunity to do this through formal work placements or part-time employment experience.

The qualification prepares learners for employment in the IT and computing sectors and will be suitable for learners who have already decided that they wish to enter this area of work. Some adult learners may wish to make the commitment required by this qualification in order to enter a specialist area of employment in IT and computing sectors or progress into higher education.

With our quality assurance process for all BTEC higher-level programmes on the QCF, we can be sure our centres are meeting national standards.
