Pearson LCCI Level 1 Certificate in Book-keeping

LCCI Level 1 qualiﬁcations improve your basic knowledge and skills in a particular subject or business-related job area, and help you to use your learning to complete straightforward routine tasks with direction and guidance.

In addition, LCCI Level 1 qualifications help you prepare for LCCI Level 2 qualiﬁcations

Entry Requirements

  • Basic Knowledge on GCE ‘O’ Level Mathematics and English Language or IGCSE Mathematics and English Language, OR
  • Completed Year 11 or Form Five of Secondary Education, OR
  • Obtained at least 1 year of work experience in related fields, OR
  • Other qualifications approved by the Brunei Darussalam National Accreditation Council (BDNAC), Ministry of Education, Brunei Darussalam

Duration

  • Part-time:       6 Months
  • Full-time:       6 Months

Modules

  • Book-keeping
  • Melayu Islam Beraja (MIB) For Level 1
  • Mathematics
  • Level 1 Language and Communication Skills (LCS)

Job Opportunities:

Accounts clerks, Travel Assistant, Hotel Receptionist
