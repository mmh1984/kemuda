Pearson LCCI Level 2 Diploma in Book-keeping and Accounting

LCCI Level 2 qualiﬁcations give you good knowledge and a solid understanding of a subject or business-related job area. They help you to use your learning to complete clearly set tasks and solve straightforward problems with some guidance or supervision. Level 2 qualiﬁcations are usually the recommended minimum entry requirements for jobs

In addition, LCCI Level 1 qualifications help you prepare for LCCI Level 2 qualiﬁcations

Entry Requirements

  • At least 2 GCE ‘O’ Level credits in any subjects and at least passes in Mathematics and Accounting or any related field, OR
  • At least 2 IGCSE ‘O’ Level credits in any subjects and at least passes in Mathematics and Accounting or any related field, OR
  • Pass Pearson LCCI Level 1 Certificate in Book-keeping or equivalent, OR
  • Other qualifications approved by the Brunei Darussalam National Accreditation Council (BDNAC), Ministry of Education, Brunei Darussalam

Duration

  • Part-time:       12 months (1 Year)
  • Full-time:       12 months (1 Year)

Modules

  • Bookkeeping (Revision)
  • Book-Keeping and Accounting (Core)
  • Cost Accounting
  • Business Statistics
  • Computerised Book-keeping
  • Melayu Islam Beraja (MIB) For Level 2
  • Level 2 Language and Communication Skills (LCS)

Job Opportunities:

Accounts Assistant, Administration Assistant, Contact Centre Operator, Marketing Assistant.
