About LCCI

London Chamber of Commerce & Industry International Qualifications (LCCI-IQ) United Kingdom (UK) is a mordern international qualification and practical. Each year over 250,000 LCCI candidates from 120 commonwealth countries use LCCI qualification to find job, improve skills, enter the university or participate in professional organization as this qualification is one of the world as prestigious qualifications.

LCCI qualifications are widely regarded by employers as preparing students to carry bout the key functions of modern international business. The qualifications are recognised worldwide by employers, universities and professional bodies such as the Association of Chartered Certified Accountants (ACCA) recognise them across the world.

Advantages of LCCI Certificate:

  • RECOGNITION
    • LCCI provides practical training and the qualifications is recognized by most companies. Professional bodies such as ACCA, CIMA, ICSA, CTIM and Universities also accept the certificate as part of their entry requirements.
  • INTERNATIONAL
    • LCCI offers qualification that is high quality, consistent and internationally recognized.
  • FLEXIBLE
    • The requirement to obtain the LCCI certificate is very flexible since their short-term module can be completed within a certain period of time
