NCC Level 5 Diploma in Business and Information Technology (L5DBIT)

The Level 5 Diploma in Computing (QCF) (L5DC) makes up the second year of the NCC Education degree journey and builds upon the knowledge gained during the NCC Education Level 4 Diploma in Computing. Exposing you to greater detail and more advanced topics in a range of areas including database development, information systems analysis, and network security and cryptography.


You will study a balance of academic and vocational subjects in order to provide you with the necessary knowledge and skills to play a significant role in IT organisations.


On successful completion of the qualification you will be able to complete the final year of a degree at one of the many universities that recognise NCC Education qualifications, or pursue a career in the IT industry.

Entry Requirements

  • At least 4 GCE ‘O’ Level credits in any subjects, OR
  • At least 1 GCE ‘A’ Level in any subjects and at least 4 GCE ‘O’ Level credits in any subjects, OR
  • At least 1 GCE ‘A’ Level in any subjects and at least 4 IGCSE ‘O’ Level at grade A* - C in any subjects, OR
  • Pass NCC Level 4 Diploma in Computing or NCC Level 4 Diploma in Business Information Technology or equivalent, OR
  • Other qualifications approved by the Brunei Darussalam National Accreditation Council (BDNAC), Ministry of Education, Brunei Darussalam

Duration

  • Full-time:       18 months (1 year 6 months)
  • Part-time:       24 months (2 years)

Modules

Core
(i) Professional Issues in IT
(ii) Information Systems Analysis
(iii) Database Design and Development
(iv) Information Systems and Organisations
(v) Principles of Business Operations
(vi) Office Solutions Development
(vii) Business IT Project
General
(viii) Melayu Islam Beraja (MIB) For Level 5
