NCC Level 4 Diploma in Business and Information Technology (L4DBIT)

Level 4 Diploma in Computing (QCF) (L4DC) makes up the first year of the NCC Education degree journey. The qualification is equivalent to the first year of an IT degree in the UK university system.

You will be given the opportunity to develop essential thinking and study skills, not only within the computing domain, but also within the context of business through a balance of academic and vocational subjects. The qualification will allow you to understand and enter the computing profession with the necessary knowledge expected within the industry and the ability to apply the skills to a range of IT- related functions.

Entry Requirements

  • At least 4 GCE ‘O’ Level credits in any subjects, OR
  • At least 4 IGCSE ‘O’ Level at grade A* - C in any subjects, OR
  • Pass NCC Level 3 Diploma in Computing or equivalent, OR
  • Other qualifications approved by the Brunei Darussalam National Accreditation Council (BDNAC), Ministry of Education, Brunei Darussalam

Duration

  • Full-time:       12 months (1 year)
  • Part-time:       18 months (1 year 6 months)

Modules

(i) Skills for Computing
(ii) Computer Networks
(iii) Computer Systems
(iv) Designing and Developing a Website
(v) Databases
(vi) eBusiness
(vii) Essentials of Management
(viii) Understanding Business Organisations
General
(ix) Melayu Islam Beraja (MIB) For Level 4
