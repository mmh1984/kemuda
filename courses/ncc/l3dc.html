NCC Level 3 Diploma in Computing (L3DC)

The NCC Education Level 3 Diploma in Computing (QCF) is an Applied General qualification which allows candidates to demonstrate key transferrable study skills, mathematical understanding and applied IT competency, as well as an understanding of the essential concepts of computer programming.

Entry Requirements

  • At least 2 GCE ‘O’ Level credits in any subjects, OR
  • At least 2 IGCSE ‘O’ Level at grade A* - C in any subjects, OR
  • Other qualifications approved by the Brunei Darussalam National Accreditation Council (BDNAC), Ministry of Education, Brunei Darussalam

Duration

  • Full-time:       9 months
  • Part-time:       9 months

Modules

Core
(i) Study and Communication Skills
(ii) Mathematical Techniques
(iii) IT Skills
(iv) Introduction to Computing
(v) Introduction to Programming

General
(vi) Melayu Islam Beraja (MIB)
