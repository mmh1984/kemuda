Overview

KEMUDA Certificate Level 1 (Entry) for Information Technology (IT) is a short course for KEMUDA Institute designed by school leavers to meet the needs of today’s businesses.

The suite of certificate qualifications allow individuals to acquire IT Skills that will help them work effectively and efficiently in their employment and this also can help to pursue for the next level on BTEC programme.

There is a basic choice of units including Microsoft application and multimedia-based applications. The KCL1 offers a flexible route to achieve the skills needed for work in future. It is a tailored programme that can meet individual needs before proceeding to BTEC Level 2 Certificate for a year programme.
