Alumni Success Stories

 

SITI MAJEEDAH BINTI HAJI ALI ASGAR


Currently pursuing her degree at Universiti Malaysia Sarawak (UNIMAS).Joined KEMUDA Institute (KI) in the year 2010 as a student in NCC International Certificate Computer Studies and successfully kept continuing to higher level up to Level 5 Diploma in Business Information Technology.

Graduated completely in the year 2014 and she was awarded the Executive Chairman Student on KI 4th Convocation Ceremony. Siti Majeedah has been actively not just only in college, but also outside curriculum activities.

Contributing herself for her country and people; as a former of President of KEMUDA Institute Student Council in 2010-2011, one of Brunei Participating Youth for the 39th Ship of Southeast ASEAN Youth Programme Ship of Southeast ASEAN Youth Programme (SSEAYP) in 2012 program sponsored by government of Japan as a small ambassador of Brunei Darussalam

Currently, she is continuing her in Degree in Human Resource Development in one of Malaysia’s top public university, University Malaysia Sarawak (UNIMAS), she has attributed to Allah SWT and expressed her gratitude to Him for giving her the strength and courage to pursue her dream. She also expressed her gratitude feelings to what she has become today were all from the backbones and guidelines of her supportive family, friends and KEMUDA Institute lecturers and staffs.
